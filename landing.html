Core capabilities

How it works

From raw signal
to deal insight.

01

Signals are ingested automatically

Every 6 hours, Arbor queries SEC EDGAR for new filings. Every 12 hours, it scans Google News and PE Wire for private asset signals. No manual monitoring required.

02

Claude extracts structured intelligence

Each raw signal is sent to Claude with a structured extraction prompt. The model returns company name, deal stage, confidence score, and the exact sentence that justified the classification.

03

Your radar updates in real time

Confirmed signals update the radar immediately. Low-confidence signals go to the analyst review queue. Your team sees only verified, sourced deal movements.
